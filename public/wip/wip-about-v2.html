Tennessee's Strongest Tradition

The Tennessee Senior Olympics, Inc. is a member of the National Senior Games Association — the largest sporting group in the United States. We're a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization endorsed by the Tennessee Recreation and Parks Association.

Our Mission Since 1981

Healthy. Active. Vital.

For over 30 years, we've worked toward a single goal — promoting healthy lifestyles for seniors through fitness, sports, and active involvement in life. Our programs contribute to the vision of healthy, active, and vital senior adults across every corner of Tennessee.

01 — Healthy

Fitness First

Competition is our catalyst for lifelong wellness. Training, moving, and showing up is what keeps our athletes thriving year after year.

02 — Active

In the Game

From the starting line to the podium, our athletes stay in motion — because an active life is the most rewarding one.

03 — Vital

Fully Alive

Sports, sportsmanship, and community — the ingredients for a vital senior life, built right here in Tennessee.

Four Decades of Growth

From 300 to 4,000 Strong

Since 1981, the Olympics have grown from 300 participants to almost 4,000 seniors competing statewide each year. Our largest State Finals attracted nearly 2,000 athletes for a week-long sports celebration.

Competition Across Tennessee

Every spring, thousands of senior adults compete in sanctioned District Olympics held in ten championship cities. Finalists advance to the State Finals each summer, and State Finals winners qualify for the Nationals held every two years.
