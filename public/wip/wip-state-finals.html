Are You Eligible?

2026 State Finals Eligibility

Per the TSO Board, participation in a 2025 or 2026 Winter District Games was required to qualify in many sports for the 2026 State Finals. This was communicated in many e-mails last year and by your district coordinators.

Looking Ahead The 2026 State Finals events are qualifiers for the 2027 Nationals set for Tulsa, OK.
  • Please study the sport lineup graphic above detailing which sports require participation at a district.
  • OPEN SPORTS exceptions: see the sport lineup graphic above.
  • Brand-new athletes just learning about the TSO are granted a one-year exemption from district play and may register for state finals events. Spread the word!
  • Athletes must be 50 years old by December 31, 2026, to compete in the 2026 state finals.

What It Costs to Compete

Entry fees support our entire non-profit 501c3 organization as we strive to promote active aging, healthy lifestyles, and improved well-being. Collectively, your fees help purchase items for the state finals such as athlete and volunteer shirts, medals, athlete insurance, equipment, signage, printing, online registration and check/credit card fees, IT costs, officials, seasonal staff help, volunteer meals and so much more for your state finals.
