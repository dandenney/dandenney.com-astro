## Overview

This page ports the Binance visual language into YIL editorial format. Binance runs on a deep near-black canvas (#0b0e11) with exactly one high-voltage accent — Binance Yellow (#FCD535) — doing all the brand's authority work. That scarcity is the system: yellow appears rarely so every use lands with force. The day's four themes each get their own accent pulled from Binance's semantic palette: guardrails gets the yellow (most important insight, most authority), nanotags gets the trading-up green (native growth, forward momentum), gamification gets the turquoise (product-minded, human-centered), and agent orchestration gets the trading-down red (interrupt states, active status, urgency). The "imperfect understanding" quote drops into an elevated surface band (#1e2329) mid-page as a philosophical pivot between the technical first half and the systems-thinking second half. Two elevation levels — canvas (#0b0e11) and surface (#1e2329) — provide all the depth; no shadows, no gradients.

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use yellow (#FCD535 / yellow-400) only twice across the entire page: once on the hero headline word "Autonomy," and then exclusively within the Guardrails section. Its scarcity is what makes it authoritative — every other occurrence would dilute both.
- Keep elevation simple and binary: main canvas (#0b0e11) vs. elevated surface (#1e2329). Cards, the quote band, the agent-view band, and the code window sit at surface level; the rest sits at canvas level. No shadows needed — the 12-step lightness gap does the separation work.
- Render the nanotags code window at a size where the markup is legible without horizontal scroll — if the example wraps badly, it undercuts the "thin and clean" character of the library being described.

### Don'ts
- Don't mix two accent colors inside a single section — each section owns one accent. The Binance system trusts a single color to do all the heavy lifting per context; layering accents inside one section splits the signal.
- Don't add atmospheric backgrounds, mesh gradients, glows, or aurora effects to any canvas surface — Binance is explicit about this. Color-block contrast does depth work; atmospheric decoration muddies the authority feel.
- Don't soften display weights — hero and section titles stay at 700. Weight 400 or 500 at display size reads as portfolio; weight 700 reads as platform.
