## harness-loop-illustration

**Subject:** An abstract geometric illustration of interconnected circuit loops and feedback cycles. Thin single-pixel lines form a network of nodes connected by curved paths suggesting: a model output node, a runtime feedback node, a validation node, and a correction loop. The network forms an elegant closed cycle rather than a linear flow.
**Style:** Technical line art, minimal. Thin white or pale green strokes on transparent background. No fills, no gradients. Geometric precision with slight organic curve at the connecting paths. Similar to Cohere's thin-line capability icons but at full-page illustration scale.
**Mood:** Quiet precision. Infrastructure-grade confidence. Not flashy — systematic.
**Palette:** Lines in white or very pale green (#7fffd4 at low opacity), on transparent or deep green background (#003c33). Nodes as simple circles or small filled dots.
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9
**Notes:** No text in image. The illustration will be placed at ~10% opacity as a background layer behind white text on a deep green (#003c33) section background.
