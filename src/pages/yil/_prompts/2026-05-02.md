# Image Prompts — YIL 2026-05-02

## hero-illustration

**Subject:** Abstract editorial illustration — overlapping geometric shapes suggesting flow, pipelines, and the gaps between process steps. Rounded rectangles and connecting lines arranged as a loose value stream map, with some gaps visually highlighted. No people, no text, no UI chrome.
**Style:** Flat / Risograph-style illustration. Clean shapes with slight texture. Not photorealistic. Not 3D.
**Mood:** Warm, optimistic, curated — the feeling of a well-designed morning newsletter. Purposeful and calm, not anxious or technical.
**Palette:** Warm amber (#d97706) and sky blue (#0284c7) as primary shape colors, on a white or very light cream background. Soft shadows or texture grain. No dark backgrounds.
**Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square) — will be displayed in a two-column hero grid on large screens
**Notes:** No text in image. No icons or logos. Should read as a background/decorative element at reduced opacity if needed. Avoid anything that looks like a flowchart or corporate diagram — lean abstract and editorial.
