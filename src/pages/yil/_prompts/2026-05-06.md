## hero-rule

**Subject:** A single horizontal hairline divider — a minimalist typographic separator element, not a photographic image. This is implemented as a CSS element (1px, #dddddd) in the hero section beneath the date stamp. No generative image needed for this placement.
**Style:** Pure CSS treatment — no image asset required
**Mood:** Clean, restrained, Airbnb-editorial calm
**Palette:** #dddddd hairline on #ffffff white canvas; accent colors #ff385c, #460479, #92174d appear only in section-level elements below
**Aspect ratio:** n/a (horizontal rule element)
**Notes:** No photographic image; the hero's visual weight comes from generous white space and the centered typographic column alone — consistent with Airbnb's principle of trusting white space over decorative imagery on concept-heavy pages
