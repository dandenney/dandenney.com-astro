Melt N Dip in St. Petersburg, FL is the kind of dessert stop that understands one simple truth: if you’re going out for waffles, you’re probably not looking for subtle. We kept it straightforward and ordered a chocolate waffle, a brownie waffle, and waters, and that ended up being exactly the right move.

Both waffles leaned hard into the maximalist lane. Big, rich, sweet, and clearly built to feel like a treat instead of a casual snack. The chocolate waffle does what you want it to do—full chocolate energy, no hedging. The brownie waffle doubles down on that same idea with an even heavier dessert vibe. If your goal is “let’s get something fun and a little over the top,” this place is on target.

The waters mattered more than expected. With this much sweetness in play, having something neutral on the table keeps the whole thing enjoyable instead of overwhelming. It also makes it easier to split bites, compare the two, and not tap out halfway through.

One caveat: this is not a light stop. If you’re craving something balanced or only mildly sweet, maximalist waffles can feel like a lot, fast. But if you go in knowing what it is, it lands.
